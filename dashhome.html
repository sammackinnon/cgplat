      2003 29,000 975 10 Tarbert

      Established in 2003 on Harris, Nan-Eileanan Siar, the NHT acquired a 29,000 hectare estate to manage on behalf of the community, currently populated by 975 people. The NHT faces challenges common to civic and governmental organisations across the Hebridean region. Namely, managing and distributing initiatives across a large but sparsely populated area efficiently while mitigating modern risks to island economies.
