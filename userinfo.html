    User information

    This digital platform aims to support community-level governance and developmental planning on commonly managed lands. The application will create a standard framework of data monitoring, data sharing and data management for all community organisations. Its aim is to both simplify the task of harnessing data for development but also make it less costly for community organisations by pooling data resources across the whole civic landowner space. 

    The final application will allow community organisations and individuals to access and share data relevant to their community lands, providing better situational awareness and enabling predictive, democratic planning using maps, spatial and other forms of data. Its applications cover a range of areas: economic, political, cultural, demographic and more. It will also aim to offer training materials for community members to use spatial data and data management tools.

    The resource you see here is simply for demonstration purposes and not the final form. It is incomplete visually and in its information. Some of the datasets included are not at the fullest resolution or accuracy.
